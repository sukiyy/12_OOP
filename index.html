OO Version Connect Four Game

Connect Four is played on a grid, 7 wide by 6 deep, with two players, first player and the second player. The players alternate turns, dropping a piece of their color in the top of a column. The piece will fall down to the further-down unoccupied slot.

Rules for winner:
Winner makes four in a row, it can be horizontally, vertically, or diagonally. The game is a tie if the entire board fills up without a winner.
